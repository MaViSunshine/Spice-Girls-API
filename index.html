Spice Girls API

For info on the group members follow this example:

localhost:8000/member/geri


For info on the albums follow this example:

localhost:8000/discography/first


To get info on an album track follow this examples:

localhost:8000/album/spice/track_01

localhost:8000/album/spiceworld/track_09

localhost:8000/album/forever/track_06
